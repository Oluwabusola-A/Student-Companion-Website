Student Companion Website

This website is designed to be a student companion, offering a reading timetable that takes into consideration both work, school, and bus schedules. With this website, students can have a more organized approach to their reading and better manage their time. The website has a database that stores all the information needed to create a personalized reading timetable, making it easier for students to keep track of their progress. The reading timetable is flexible, taking into consideration the different schedules of each student, allowing them to fit in their reading and other activities in their busy schedules.
